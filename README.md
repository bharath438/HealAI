🏥 HealAI – Smart Healthcare Navigator & Universal Health Assistant
🚀 Overview

HealAI is an AI-powered healthcare platform designed to make healthcare affordable, accessible, and intelligent for both humans and animals.

It helps users:

Understand symptoms
Estimate treatment costs
Find hospitals & doctors
Access government schemes
Get Ayurvedic + modern health suggestions

👉 Built for real-world impact in India, especially rural and underserved areas.

🎯 Problem Statement

Healthcare today is:

Expensive 💸
Complex 🤯
Hard to access in rural regions 🌾

Additionally:

Lack of cost transparency
Poor awareness of government schemes
Neglected animal healthcare

👉 This leads to delayed treatment and higher risks.

💡 Solution

HealAI provides a unified AI-driven healthcare assistant that:

Guides users through healthcare decisions
Predicts treatment costs
Suggests personalized remedies
Supports both human and animal health
